# Company Handbook — Silver Tier

## Enhancements Over Bronze
- Structured CRM tracking required
- Invoice IDs must follow format: INV-YYYYMM-###
- Every client must exist in /CRM/Clients.md

## Approval Rules
- External emails require approval
- Invoices require approval
- CRM updates auto-approve (internal only)

## Logging
Every action must append JSON entry in /Logs/YYYY-MM-DD.json
